Overview

Every 3D viewport in 3D File Tools has a floating toolbar on the left side of the canvas. The toolbar is collapsible, repositionable, and contains buttons for camera control, view options, and (in the 3D Viewer) model transforms.

Toolbar Controls

Toolbar Toggle

ButtonIconDescription
Toggle ToolbarChevron right/leftCollapses or expands the toolbar body. When collapsed, only the toggle button is visible. All tool buttons inside are hidden.

Axis Gizmo

A small canvas at the top of the toolbar displays the current X (red), Y (green), and Z (blue) axis directions relative to the camera. It updates continuously as you orbit. The 3D Viewer uses a 44×44 px gizmo; the Plate Placer and Integra Generator use a 28×28 px compact version.

Camera Controls

ButtonIconDescription
Reset CameraVideo cameraReturns the camera to its default position and angle for the loaded model. The target point is reset to the model center.
Zoom to FitExpand arrowsRepositions and zooms the camera so the entire model fills the viewport. Works in both perspective and orthographic modes.

View Mode Controls

ButtonIconDescription
WireframeDraw polygonToggles wireframe rendering on/off. In wireframe mode, solid surfaces are replaced by edge lines. Toggles state is remembered per-viewport.
Perspective / OrthographicCubeToggles between perspective projection (natural 3D) and orthographic projection (no perspective foreshortening). Useful for precise alignment and measurement.
GridBorder-all (with slash overlay when off)Toggles the reference grid plane on/off. The grid is rendered at the model base and adapts to the model scale.

Zoom & Pan

ButtonIconDescription
Zoom InPlus (round)Zooms the camera in by a fixed step
Zoom OutMinus (round)Zooms the camera out by a fixed step
Pan ModeHand / palmEnables pan mode. In pan mode, left-click drag pans the camera instead of orbiting. Click again to disable. The button is highlighted while active.

Transform Toggle (3D Viewer Only)

ButtonIconDescription
Rotate / MirrorSync/rotate arrowsOpens or closes the Transform flyout panel. The flyout contains 6 rotation buttons (±90° around X/Y/Z) and 3 mirror buttons (X/Y/Z). See 3D Viewer for details.

Ruler (Integra Generator Only)

ButtonIconDescription
RulerRulerEnables the measurement ruler. Click two points on the model to measure the 3D distance between them. Press X/Y/Z to lock the axis. Press Esc to cancel.

Drag Handle

ElementIconDescription
Drag HandleGrip horizontal dotsDrag this handle to reposition the entire toolbar anywhere within the viewport. The toolbar position is not persisted between page reloads.
